# Vacation Policy

Full-time employees accrue 18 days of paid vacation per calendar year, credited
in full on January 1st. Vacation days do not roll over between calendar years;
any unused balance is forfeited on December 31st.

Time off requests must be submitted through the HR portal at least 5 business
days before the requested start date. Manager approval is required for all
requests, and requests spanning more than 10 consecutive business days
additionally require sign-off from the department director.

# Expense Reimbursement

Employees may submit expense reports for business travel, client meals, and
approved software subscriptions. Reimbursement requests must include an
itemized receipt and be submitted within 30 days of the expense.
Reimbursements are processed on the last business day of each month.

Meals during business travel are reimbursed up to $75 per day. Alcohol is not
reimbursable under any circumstances.

# Remote Work Policy

Employees may work remotely up to 3 days per week with manager approval.
Employees requesting a fully remote arrangement need VP-level sign-off,
reviewed annually every January.
