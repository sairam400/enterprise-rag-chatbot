Password Policy

All employee accounts must use a password of at least 14 characters,
including one number and one symbol. Passwords must be rotated every 180 days
and cannot repeat any of the last 5 passwords used.

Multi-factor authentication is required for all accounts with access to
production systems or customer data. Hardware security keys are the required
second factor for engineers with production database access; app-based
authenticators are acceptable for all other roles.

Incident Reporting

Any suspected security incident, including phishing emails, lost devices, or
unauthorized access, must be reported to security@company.example within 1
hour of discovery. The security team's target acknowledgment time is 15
minutes during business hours.

Data Classification

Customer data is classified as Confidential and may only be accessed by
employees with an active business need, logged through the access request
system. Publicly available marketing material is classified as Public and has
no access restrictions.
